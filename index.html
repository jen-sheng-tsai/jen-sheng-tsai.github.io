Jen-Sheng Tsai 蔡仁勝

Jen-Sheng Tsai is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Electrical Engineering, National University of Kaohsiung (NUK), Taiwan, since 2023. He received his B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in computer science and information engineering from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), Taiwan. His research interests include artificial internet of things, edge computing, artificial intelligence, embedding system, information security.
